## If a Video Shows "Unavailable"
Some videos may become unavailable due to:
- YouTube embedding restrictions
- Region blocking
- Video being deleted or made private
- Copyright issues

**Solution:**
1. Click the **"YouTube" button** to watch directly on YouTube
2. Click **"Show Image"** to see the static exercise image instead
3. The image + written instructions are always available as backup
